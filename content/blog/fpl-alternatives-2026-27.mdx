## Fantrax draft leagues

Every player belongs to one manager only. Once your rival drafts the obvious captain pick, he's gone, and template teams become impossible. [Fantrax](https://www.fantrax.com/fantasy/games/season-long/epl) is free for standard PL draft leagues and endlessly customisable.

The catch: draft leagues need six to twelve of you properly committed, every week, all season. A dead draft league is sadder than a bad FPL rank.

**Verdict: the deepest fix if your league is reliable. Flaky friends need not apply.**

## Sorare

Fantasy where the players are digital cards you own and trade. On [Sorare](https://sorare.com) your squad carries over between seasons instead of resetting every August, which is genuinely different.

But it's a money game at the competitive end. Card prices move with form and hype, and you're half manager, half trader whether you like it or not.

**Verdict: for collectors who enjoy the market as much as the football.**

## Sky Fantasy Football

[Sky's game](https://fantasyfootball.skysports.com/) is free, familiar in format, and has historically offered up to £50,000 for the overall winner. That's a lot more than FPL pays for a season of your life.

It's still the same genre though. Same budget as everyone, same value picks, same lookalike leader pack by Christmas. Check the site for confirmed 2026/27 prizes before you plan the celebration.

**Verdict: change of scenery, not a change of game.**

## Telegraph Fantasy Football and Dream Team

The newspaper games. Simpler than FPL, five minutes of admin a week, and [Dream Team](https://www.dreamteamfc.com/) advertised a £60,000 top prize last season. Neither had confirmed 2026/27 details when we published, so treat prize figures as last season's.

Less to manage also means less separating you from the bloke who set his team in August and vanished.

**Verdict: casual money shots. Fine as a side bet.**

## UCL Fantasy

[UEFA's official game](https://gaming.uefa.com/en/uclfantasy/) is free and well made. Short bursts around Champions League nights, from September. Forces you to actually learn other leagues.

The calendar is the limit: matchday clusters with long gaps. Works as a second game, struggles as your main one.

**Verdict: the European nights side dish.**

## The rest, quickly

Kickbase covers Bundesliga, LaLiga and MLS, not the Premier League, so it's off the list for UK players. [Draft Fantasy](https://www.draftfantasy.com/) does auction drafts well. FanTeam is real-money with all the 18+ caveats. FPL's own Draft and Challenge modes are free lighter twists inside the official ecosystem.
