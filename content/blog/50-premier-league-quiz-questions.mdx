## Round 1: The warm-up (if you miss these, close the tab)

The freebies. A tourist gets seven, a real fan gets ten and feels slightly insulted.

1. In what year did the Premier League play its very first season?
2. Which club has won the most Premier League titles?
3. Who is the Premier League's all-time record goalscorer, with 260 goals?
4. Which club went the entire 2003/04 league season unbeaten?
5. "AGUEROOOOO!" Sergio Aguero's 93:20 winner sealed the 2012 title for Manchester City against which opponents?
6. How many clubs compete in the Premier League each season?
7. Leicester City won the 2015/16 title at 5000/1. Who was their manager?
8. Which club left Highbury for the Emirates Stadium in 2006?
9. Erling Haaland joined Manchester City in 2022 from which club?
10. Who won the 2025/26 Premier League title, their first in 22 years?

### Round 1 answers

1. 1992 (the 1992/93 season)
2. Manchester United, with 13
3. Alan Shearer
4. Arsenal, the Invincibles
5. QPR
6. 20
7. Claudio Ranieri
8. Arsenal
9. Borussia Dortmund
10. Arsenal, confirmed champions in May 2026

19. Petr Cech holds the record for the most Premier League clean sheets. Higher or lower than 200?
20. Which goalkeeper did David Beckham lob from inside his own half on the opening day of the 1996/97 season?
