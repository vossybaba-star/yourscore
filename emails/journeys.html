📥

Away-events only

Automated emails fire for things that happened while the user wasn't looking. We never email someone about points they just watched themselves score.

🔂

Most fire once, ever

16–21 are first-time-only (server checks "is this actually the first?"). 23 is hard-capped at one send per user via the email_log table.

📅

~2 per week ceiling

The recurring lane (05–08, 10) is throttled to matchdays + a single Sunday digest. No daily blasts — that's push's job once mobile lands.

🚪

Every email has the exit

One-click List-Unsubscribe header + footer pause/unsubscribe on all 23. Pausing is scoped (this league / all).

How to read it: a new user always gets 01 Welcome, then drops into whichever lane matches their first action — they don't get all of them. A 38-0-first player never sees the quiz nudges, and vice-versa. The social lane (20–21) and the dormant lane (23) cut across everyone. "Friends online" is deliberately absent — that's a push notification, not an email (no last_seen infra, and email is too slow to be useful for presence).  ·  Hand-maintained alongside the templates; regenerate the gallery with node scripts/preview-emails.mjs.
