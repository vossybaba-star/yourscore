# YourScore Leaderboard — Marketing Explainer (v2, LIVE)

> Source of truth for scripts, ads, and social copy about the ranking system.
> Replaces the earlier percentile/tier explainer — **do not use "better than X%",
> "Platinum tier", or any percentage framing. Positions and points only.**
> System shipped 2026-06-12 (migration 30). Mechanics confirmed against prod.

## The one-liner
**More points, higher up the table. One leaderboard. One #1.**

## The 30-second version
Everything you play in YourScore earns points on **one leaderboard**:

- 🧠 **Knowledge points** — from quizzes and solo challenges. Know your football, score points.
- ⚽ **Match points** — from 38-0. **A win is worth 1,500 points. A draw, 500.** Just like a real table — winning is everything.

Add them together and that's **your YourScore**. The leaderboard sorts it. Your position is your status — and there's only ever **one #1**.

## How to explain the numbers (if asked)
*"Why is a quiz worth hundreds and a win worth 1,500?"* — Because they're balanced per
session: **one 38-0 win ≈ one strong quiz session.** Whichever game you love, every game
you play moves you up. Play both, climb twice as fast.

## The hooks (use these)
- **Every game counts.** Finish any game — quiz or match — and you see exactly what you earned and where you now sit.
- **The chase.** The app tells you who's directly above you and how many points you need: *"320 pts behind Dave (#13) — overtake him."* The target is always a *person*, not a number.
- **Your score never goes down.** Points only stack. Stop playing and the only risk is your mates overtaking you.
- **One #1.** No ties, no sharing. Somebody is top of the table right now — and it could be you.
- **Friends table.** A private leaderboard of just your mates. Bragging rights, settled daily.
- **Badges** (cosmetic flavour, position-based): 👑 #1 · Elite top 10 · 💎 Diamond top 50 · Platinum top 200 · Gold top 1000.

## Accuracy guardrails (internal)
- ❌ No percentages ("better than 72%") — removed from the product.
- ❌ No point-based tiers ("reach 800 points for Diamond") — badges come from *position*.
- ❌ No "rank resets every season/week" — ranks are all-time today (no seasonal reset yet).
- ❌ Never say "football IQ" — always "football knowledge".
- ✅ Win = 1,500 / Draw = 500 is safe to state publicly (it's shown in-app).
- ⚠️ Hold off celebrating named top players publicly until bot-win farming is reviewed
  (wins vs matchmaking bots currently count toward Match points).
