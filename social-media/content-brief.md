## CAROUSEL POSTS — 2-SLIDE QUIZ FORMAT

### Format Rules
- **Slide 1:** The question. Big, bold, clean. No answer. Ends with "Swipe for the answer →"
- **Slide 2:** The answer revealed. Context sentence explaining why it's interesting. Fake engagement stat (see below). CTA to yourscore.app.
- **Format:** 4:5 (1080×1350px) for both slides

### The Stat Psychology
The fake engagement stat on Slide 2 makes the viewer feel smarter than average. Rules:
- Always skew the "got it right" % DOWN — if a question is genuinely 40% knowable, show 22%
- Pool size: specific, non-round numbers — never over 500, never a round hundred
- Name the most common wrong answer ("most people said Norway") — this validates the viewer who got it right
- The viewer who got it wrong still shares it because the answer is interesting
